Completed the **Google UX Design Professional Certificate**, an 8-course program designed to build job-ready skills in user experience design, from research and ideation to high-fidelity prototyping and testing.

## Skills Gained

Mastered the complete UX design process including **user research**, **wireframing**, **prototyping in Figma**, **usability testing**, and **responsive design** for web and mobile interfaces.

## Course Curriculum

- Foundations of User Experience (UX) Design
- Start the UX Design Process: Empathize, Define, and Ideate
- Build Wireframes and Low-Fidelity Prototypes
- Conduct UX Research and Test Early Concepts
- Create High-Fidelity Designs and Prototypes in Figma
- Build Dynamic User Interfaces (UI) for Websites
- Design a User Experience for Social Good & Prepare for Jobs
- Accelerate Your Job Search with AI

## Key Takeaways

This certification emphasized hands-on portfolio projects applying design thinking methodologies and human-centered design principles. Gained proficiency in industry-standard tools like Figma and Adobe XD, while building a professional portfolio of case studies demonstrating end-to-end UX design processes.
