Completed the **Google IT Support Professional Certificate**, a 5-course program designed to prepare learners for entry-level IT support roles with comprehensive training in troubleshooting, networking, security, and system administration.

## Skills Gained

Built foundational skills in **technical troubleshooting**, **computer networking**, **operating systems** (Windows and Linux), **IT security**, **system administration**, and **customer service** in technical environments.

## Course Curriculum

- Technical Support Fundamentals
- The Bits and Bytes of Computer Networking
- Operating Systems and You: Becoming a Power User
- System Administration and IT Infrastructure Services
- IT Security: Defense Against the Digital Dark Arts

## Key Takeaways

This certification provided hands-on experience with real-world IT scenarios including hardware and software troubleshooting, network configuration, user account management, and implementing security best practices. Developed critical thinking skills for diagnosing technical issues and communicating solutions effectively to non-technical users.
